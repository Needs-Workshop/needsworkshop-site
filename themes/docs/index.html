What is Needs Workshop?

Needs Workshop is a way to find new perspectives, common understanding and effective ways of making progress in groups or organisations.

Every organisation on earth does the same thing - they all flow value from ‘please’ to ‘thank you’. Needs Workshop reveals the things that impede this flow and supports you in tackling them.

Having developed approaches to resolve systemic issues and improve flow within software development organisations, we’re now applying this to all kinds of organisation that do knowledge work

Get everyone in your team, department or entire company on the same page immediately

How do we work?

We use group facilitation, along with a consulting approach based on humble inquiry.

Facilitation

Traditional business meetings are notiriously ineffective, leaving the attendees feeling deflated or frustrated.

We facilitate productive and inclusive meetings or workshops for groups of all sizes, from small numbers up to very large scale, limited only by physical space available

(Humble) Consulting

You are the expert on you, and everything you need to know in order to change and is in your own organisation if you have the ability to see it.

Our role is to help you find the answers. We use a consulting approach based on a technique called humble inquiry, allowing your teams to find and own their solutions
